Who is Setsuko Yokokawa?

Setsuko Yokokawa Setsuko Yokokawa Species Human Age 4 Gender Female First Appearance Grave of the Fireflies Voice actor (Japanese) Ayano Shirashi Voice actor (CPM) Rhoda Chrosite Voice actor (Sentai) Emily Neves Setsuko Yokokawa (横川 節子 Yokokawa Setsuko) is the deuteragonist in the Studio Ghibli film Grave of the Fireflies. She was the younger sister of Seita Yokokawa who was born in late 1943, but she later dies in 1945 towards the end of the film.
